Welcome to Viva la vida Cafe!!!

Viva la vida means 'live life' and our cafe stands to this meaning. Our cafe provides it's customers a home like place to be relax. Viva la vida cafe serves it's customers with freash and teasty coffee and snacks.

Viva la vida cafe is not just a normal cafe that sells drinks and snacks, but helps the local businesses to grow.  We everyday buy freash pastreis and coffee beans from the local vendors, which helps them with their seals and our customers gets freash, healthy, and teasty treat.

Our cafe also hosts many events, so that our customers can enjoy. Events like games night, love night, single night, comedy night and many more fun events we host for our lovely customers. The main attraction of our cafe is the charity event that we host weekly. Through this event we try to help people who are in need of our help.

Everyday here at Viva la vida cafe we keeps on improving our services and try to make Viva la vida cafe a warm and cozy place to go to.
